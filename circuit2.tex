KVL at $v_1$
\begin{gather*}
	\frac{v_1 - 8}{3k} + \frac{v_1-V_o}{6k} + 2mA = 0 \\ 
	2v_1-16 + v_1-V_o = -12 \\ 
	3v_1 + -V_o = 4
\end{gather*}

KVL at $v_2$
\begin{gather*}
	\frac{v_2-10}{5k} - 2m + \frac{v_2 - v_3}{4k} = 0 \\ 
	4v_2 - 40 - 40 + 5v_2 - 5v_3 = 0 \\
	9v_2 - 5v_3 = 80
\end{gather*}

Constrain equation supernode
\[
	V_o - 12 = v_3 \leftrightarrow - v_3 + V_o= 12
\]

KVL at supernode
\begin{gather*}
	\frac{v_3 - v_2}{4k} + \frac{V_o - v_1}{6k} + \frac{V_o}{6k} \\ 
	3v_3 - 3v_2 + 2V_o - 2v_1 + 2V_o = 0 \\
	-2v_1 - 3v_2 + 3v_3 + 4V_o = 0
\end{gather*}

Solving the matrix:
\begin{gather*}
	\begin{bmatrix}
		3 & 0 & 0 & -1 \\
		0 & 9 & -5 & 0  \\
		0 & 0 & -1 & 1 \\
		-2 & -3 & 3 & 4 \\
	\end{bmatrix}
	\begin{bmatrix}
		v_1 \\ v_2 \\ v_3 \\ V_o
	\end{bmatrix}
	=
	\begin{bmatrix}
		4 \\ 80 \\ 12 \\ 0
	\end{bmatrix}
\end{gather*}

Solving gives:
\[
	v_1 = 4\frac{4}{7}V, v_2 = 7\frac{13}{21}V, v_3 = 2\frac{2}{7}V, V_o = 9\frac{5}{7}V
\]
